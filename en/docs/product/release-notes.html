Updates 2025

On this page, we publish ongoing information about product news. We started this in March 2025.

Kvipp and Integration - Automatic transfer to Kompetanseregisteret - 25.08.2025
Automatic transfer of equipment-specific training
When a person with the role of "instructor" provides practical training to any of your employees, the result is automatically uploaded to Kompetanseregisteret. This includes both internal training and training received from rental companies, manufacturers, and others who use Kvipp AND have registered instructors.

Why integration?
  • No manual registration required
  • Ongoing documentation of equipment-specific training
  • Visible in HMSREG via Kompetanseregisteret

Complete solution
The integration automatically handles both Norwegian and foreign workers, includes training certificates (PDF) and equipment information (brand and model).

Contact us to activate the integration
Call us at 69 83 85 00 or send an email to support@digiquip.no to activate the integration or if you have any questions.
Kvipp - Various improvements in usage and look-and-feel - 01.07.2025
In June, we made a lot of small improvements to the look and feel. Here are some examples.

  • More precise text for uploading previous equipment-specific training
  • Improved how Kvipp interprets ID cards. For HMS cards, for example, you can both scan the QR code and take a picture of your name on the back of the card
  • Table improvements in Kvipp Company
  • Standardization of design and design guide
  • Retrieve training certificates from groups

PS: In addition, we have been working on a new feature that will make training much easier! More information will come after the summer holidays! 👍
Kvipp - Forward documentation by email - 29.04.2025
Email
When people with the "instructor" role have provided training, these sessions appear in lessons. We have added a function to send the document (PDF) by email. Select the people you wish to send documentation on behalf of and add the desired recipient

Kvipp - Search for people or equipment in a lesson - 02.04.2025
Search in lessons
When people with the "instructor" role have provided training, these sessions appear in training lessons. We have added the ability to search for equipment and people to make it easier to find information afterwards. This is particularly relevant for training sessions with external participants, e.g. when a machine supplier provides training to a customer

Kvipp - Groups and QR codes, adding people manually, training sessions and search - 03/31/2025
QR code for groups
Generate and print a QR code that applies to multiple equipment and people added to a group. Go to groups and select "KVIPP ID". You can print and hang this QR code, and when someone scans it, the person gets all the equipment in the group. When you provide training, you can also use the QR code to add all the people from the group.

Adding people manually to Kvipp Admin
Several people have requested the ability to add people manually in Kvipp Admin. We have added a function where you can search by first name, last name and date of birth - see the "+" button on the people page. Important: The person must have identified themselves via kvipp.it first to appear in the search. This way, there is more control over ensuring that the correct people are added.

Search for participants and equipment types in training sessions
With the role of "instructor", all training sessions end up in an overview. The overview includes all the people the instructor has trained - both internal and external. To retrieve who has received training, you can search by participant name and equipment type.
DigiQuip-Inspection Date: 18.03.2025
Unlock Function
Administrators and the original inspector can now unlock completed inspections.
Save and Close Function
Inspections that are not completed are automatically saved as drafts.
Drafts have the status "Not Inspected" and will not appear on the QR code landing page until finalized.
Pre-filling "OK" in Checklists
A new toggle function allows users to enable or disable the pre-filling of "OK" in checklists.
This provides better control if an inspection is not completed immediately but needs to be continued later. More info can be found here.
Retrieving Documents from BraReg
All documents related to the equipment and production year are now fetched directly from BraReg.
Bug Fixes
Corrected status in DigiQuip when equipment is set to "Not Approved" due to a Level 2 defect.
Fixed an issue preventing some equipment from properly linking to BraReg.
Resolved a synchronization issue for inspections from BraReg that already had an existing connection.
