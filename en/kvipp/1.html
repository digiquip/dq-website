Training in the use of
machinery and tools.

For safe and correct use

Those who will use machinery and tools must be familiar with the equipment and know how to use it correctly. Training should include, among other things:

  • Operation
  • Safety equipment
  • Area of use
  • Functionality
  • Personal protective equipment
  • Daily inspection

and this must be documented (see regulations on the execution of work).

What does the Norwegian Labour Inspection Authority require?

«Documentation showing what requirements are set for persons who can use work equipment that may pose a particular risk of injury to life or health.»

and

«Documentation showing that employees have undergone training on the specific work equipment they are assigned to use.»

And if you have this in place, you're good to go.

Choose the solution that suits you

Equipment-specific training occasionally

Use Kvipp completely free of charge! All you need is a mobile phone and an HSE card (or other ID).

  • Go to the website kvipp.it
  • Identify person with HSE card or driver's license. Scan the QR code on the HSE card or take a picture of the driver's license and let artificial intelligence do the job
  • Scan identifier (QR code/NFC tag) or use artificial intelligence to recognize the equipment

Price: Free

Only equipment-specific training

You have control over most things, but not equipment-specific training? Then you can use Kvipp and the simplest package.

  • Employees are automatically and securely added to Kvipp Bedrift via the Norwegian Labour Inspection Authority's HSE card API
  • Equipment and documentation are automatically registered after training is completed
  • Overview and gap analyses in real time. Get ongoing overview of who has or lacks training

Price: From 1390,- per month

Documented and equipment-specific training

End-to-end control of training on machinery and tools? You can use Kvipp to solve documentation of the entire process from categories to completed training, both documented safety and equipment-specific training.

Control from A to Z:

  • Categorization, with or without artificial intelligence
  • Risk assessment and measures
  • Assessment of the need for equipment-specific training
  • Requirements for training before use of machinery and tools
  • Conduct and document safety training
  • The system ensures that those concerned automatically get precautions and training requirements in Kvipp

Price: From 2490,- per month

Competence Registry (K-REG)

Equipment-specific training can be uploaded directly to K-REG, either as an add-on or standalone. K-REG also shares competence with HMSREG.

✓Visible in HMSREG via the Competence Registry
✓No manual data registration required
✓Ongoing documentation of equipment-specific training
