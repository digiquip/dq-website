Our European Software Pledge

Europe has a lot to be proud of, and we are proud to be part of it. After years of building software and mindlessly subscribing to SaaS services, we are now taking a step back and evaluating our choices. The result is that DigiQuip AS is actively working to switch our SaaS subscriptions to European alternatives wherever possible. This is a work in progress, and we will update this page as we switch services.
