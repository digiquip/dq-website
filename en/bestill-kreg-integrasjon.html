Integration with K-REG

Equipment-specific training can be uploaded directly to K-REG, either as an add-on to Kvipp Bedrift or as a standalone solution. Furthermore, K-REG shares competence with HMSREG.

✓ Visible in HMSREG via Kompetanseregisteret
✓ No manual data entry required
✓ Ongoing documentation of equipment-specific training

How to do it

SCAN HMS CARD: Automatic connection to company. Via the Arbeidstilsynets API, Kvipp retrieves the correct name, date of birth and company. This ensures the documentation is correct from the start!

SCAN EQUIPMENT: Scan QR code (Reginn, DigiQuip) or take a picture of the equipment. From the image, artificial intelligence interprets brand and model. The only thing you need to do is confirm or change the name. PS: Make sure to take a picture where both brand and model are captured.

When training is given, ALL necessary information and documentation (PDF) is transferred to K-REG. Who has received and given training, which equipment (brand and model) and date.

Only practical training given by people with the instructor role is transferred. We'll fix this when you activate the integration.
